┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                        ANALYSIS METRICS                                 │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

SPEAKING METRICS:
-----------------
1. Pace (WPM)
   - Ideal: 120-160 WPM
   - Too Slow: < 100 WPM
   - Too Fast: > 180 WPM

2. Confidence Score (0-100)
   - High: 80-100 (Very confident)
   - Medium: 60-79 (Moderately confident)
   - Low: 0-59 (Needs improvement)

3. Filler Words
   Detected: um, uh, like, you know, actually, basically,
             literally, sort of, kind of
   - Count per answer
   - Percentage of total words
   - Trend across interview

CONTENT METRICS:
----------------
1. Relevance
   - Does answer address the question?
   - On-topic vs. off-topic

2. Structure (STAR Method)
   - Situation: Context setting
   - Task: Problem definition
   - Action: What you did
   - Result: Outcome achieved

3. Clarity
   - Coherence
   - Logical flow
   - Clear communication

4. Depth
   - Technical detail (for technical interviews)
   - Specific examples
   - Quantified results

FINAL SCORES:
-------------
1. Overall Score (0-10)
   - 55% LLM semantic analysis
   - 45% Local metrics

2. Subscores
   - Technical Depth (0-10)
   - Communication Skills (0-10)
   - Problem-Solving (0-10)
   - Cultural Fit (0-10)
